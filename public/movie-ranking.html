The Best TV Shows According to Moe

My top 3 TV shows of all-time

Hidden love

This show is a beautiful love story that is captured in a brilliant cinematic way. The actors have also put in their best and you feel the journey of their love while watching the show.

Alchemy of Souls

This is a love story surrounding a powerful supernatural couple and the actors showcased the development of their love quite well. The actors are also very talented and executed the vision of the writers perfectly.

Love like the Galaxy

This show is a brilliant masterpiece. The show has romance,action, tragedy, drama,suspense and beautiful costume work. The actors are also very talented and portray the entire story perfectly. This was a wonderful casting from the directors and no other actors would have been able to perform like they did.
